1. Pick A Nation.

Emerem

The Emerem are the most powerful of the rainforest nations, and the only nation that refuses to cooperate with the forest spirits. Now, their prince is dying, and his son the prince apparent has been abducted by a mighty spirit of frost. Fate seems grim.

Emerem Features:

  • You speak the language of the rainforest spirits.

Magisterium

The Magisterium Magnifica is the imperial arm of the distant academy-city of Wisdon. The Magisterium's wizards have toppled countless kingdoms from aboard their sky ships, and now their scouts have arrived in the rainforest. You're one of them.

Magisterium Features:

  • You are fully literate in the language of magic.
  • You have two loyal indentured servants (4 Light, 5 Flesh). They carry your inherited library: 10 Grimoires with random words.

Characters of both nations speak the common tongue of the local region.

3. Decide Your Stats.

You start with 4 Light and 4 Flesh, and 4 points to assign freely.

4. The Party Must Pick a Shared Position on the Frostbearer.

  1. The Emerem should control it (as they always have).
  2. The Magisterium should control it.
  3. It must be destroyed.

5. Choose starting equipment.

You can take as much as you can carry, items fill one Flesh Slot.
