Frostbearer Character Creation Guide

The module is a small but dense hexcrawl, set in a rainforest. An ancient evil has recently awoken, kidnapped the prince, and threatens to end the forest in icy oblivion!
Will our brave heroes storm the Fortress of the Frostbearer and defeat the evil? You decide!
This an extended playtest for my system Veracity TTRPG. The gameplay involves OSR dungeon-crawling and faction play, but with an emphasis on learning and revealing secrets, exposing lies & conspiracies.
The plan is to run the campaign for at least ten weeks guaranteed, every Saturday at 16:00 UTC. I may also run other slots, depending on interest and availability. After ten weeks, the Frostbearer's influence unbalances the environment and it will begin to collapse. After that: at the end of each session, there's a 1-in-6 chance that it destroys all life in the forest. This ends the campaign.

1. Pick A Nation.

Emerem

The Emerem are the most powerful of the rainforest nations. Where the other nations live in union with the spirits, the Emerem subjugate them and treat them like pets. Now, their prince is dying, and his son the prince apparent has been abducted by a mighty spirit of frost. Fate seems grim.

Emerem Features:

  • You speak the language of the rainforest spirits.

Magisterium

The Magisterium Magnifica is the imperial arm of the distant academy-city of Wisdon. The Magisterium's wizards have toppled countless kingdoms from aboard their sky ships, and now their scouts have arrived in the rainforest. You're one of them.

Magisterium Features:

  • You are fully literate in the language of magic.
  • You have two loyal indentured servants (4 Light, 5 Flesh). They carry your inherited library: 10 Grimoires with random words.

3. Decide Your Stats.

You start with 4 Light and 4 Flesh, and 4 points to assign freely.

4. The Party Must Pick a Shared Position on the Frostbearer.

  1. The Emerem should control it (as they always have).
  2. The Magisterium should control it.
  3. It must be destroyed.

5. Choose starting equipment.

You can take as much as you can carry, items fill one Flesh Slot.
